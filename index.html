About Us

Pradeep Kumar is a cybersecurity enthusiast, ethical hacker, and full-stack developer with expertise in penetration testing, bug bounty, IoT security, and mobile application development. With certifications from UC Councilor, TryHackMe, and Java Certification, he has a strong foundation in cybersecurity and ethical hacking. He is the founder of CyberSwipe, a platform dedicated to spreading cybersecurity awareness and organizing technical events. His work focuses on researching vulnerabilities, developing secure applications, and educating people about cyber threats. Beyond cybersecurity, Pradeep has hands-on experience in Android app development, API testing, blockchain basics, and full-stack web development. He is also passionate about building automation tools, exploring new tech trends, and continuously improving his English communication skills.
